## Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how Lang Study ("Lang Study", "we", "us", or
"our") uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies on
getlangstudy.com and related learning tools.

Cookies and browser storage help the product sign users in, remember
preferences, protect sessions, measure reliability, and keep local learning
progress available on the same device. This policy should be read together with
our [Privacy Policy](/privacy), which explains how we handle personal
information more broadly.

## What Cookies and Similar Technologies Are

Cookies are small text files that a website can store in your browser. Similar
technologies include local storage, session storage, software development kits,
analytics scripts, pixels, and server logs.

Some of these technologies are needed for the service to work. Others help us
understand product usage, diagnose issues, remember settings, or improve the
learning experience.

## Technologies We Use

Lang Study may use the following categories of cookies and browser storage.

### Essential Cookies and Security Storage

These are required to provide the service or keep it secure. They may support:

- sign-in sessions, account authentication, and email verification
- fraud, spam, abuse, and security protections
- request routing, load handling, and Cloudflare security services
- account deletion, password reset, and other account workflows

If you block essential cookies, account features, paid access, or security
features may not work correctly.

### Preference Storage

We may store preferences so the product feels consistent when you return. This
can include:

- language selection
- theme or appearance settings
- interface state such as sidebar visibility
- recently selected worksheet settings

Clearing browser storage may reset these preferences.

### Local Learning Progress

Some learning features save progress in your browser rather than in an account.
For example, the beginner Chinese character practice flow may store completed
characters, mistake counts, missed strokes, review queue items, and worksheet
selections on your device.

This local progress helps you continue practice on the same browser. It may not
sync across devices unless a signed-in progress feature is later enabled.
Clearing local storage can delete this progress.

### Analytics and Product Improvement

We may use analytics tools to understand whether learners can find, use, and
complete practice flows. Depending on configuration, analytics providers may
include Cloudflare analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Umami, Google Analytics, or
Plausible.

Analytics may process information such as page URLs, referrers, browser and
device type, approximate region, interaction events, performance data, error
data, and technical identifiers. We use this information to improve reliability,
usability, conversion, and product direction.

Where consent is required for analytics cookies or similar technologies, we
will rely on consent or provide the controls required by applicable law.

### Payment, Email, and Support Services

If paid checkout, transactional email, or support tools are enabled, providers
such as Creem, Stripe, Cloudflare Email, Resend, or a support widget may use
their own cookies or similar technologies to process payments, prevent fraud,
deliver emails, provide support, or keep their services secure.

Those providers process information under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Advertising Cookies

Lang Study does not currently use advertising cookies, social media tracking
cookies, or third-party targeting cookies to build advertising profiles. We do
not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral
advertising as those terms are commonly used in U.S. state privacy laws.

If this changes, we will update this policy and provide any required notices or
choices before using such technologies.

## Managing Cookies and Browser Storage

You can manage cookies and browser storage through your browser settings. Most
browsers let you:

- view, block, or delete cookies
- clear local storage and site data
- block third-party cookies
- use private browsing modes
- set privacy preferences or tracking protections

You can also use privacy controls provided by analytics providers where
available.

Blocking or deleting storage can affect how Lang Study works. You may be signed
out, lose local learning progress, reset worksheet preferences, or need to
reconfigure product settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as Lang Study adds languages, learning tools,
account features, payments, analytics, or other services. The updated version
will be posted on this page with an updated date.
