## Overview

Lang Study helps learners practice writing systems, starting with Chinese
characters, stroke order, guided tracing, and printable worksheets. This policy
explains what information we collect, why we use it, and how to contact us.

## Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate and improve the product:

- **Account information**: If you create an account, we collect the name, email
  address, password credentials, and authentication records needed to let you
  sign in and manage your account.
- **Learning activity**: The free practice flow may save progress in your
  browser so the current lesson can continue where you left off. Future signed-in
  features may save review history, custom character lists, worksheet settings,
  and mistake history to your account.
- **Contact information**: If you email us or use a contact channel, we receive
  the information you choose to send, such as your email address and message.
- **Usage and device data**: We may process basic technical data such as pages
  visited, request logs, IP address, browser type, device type, and approximate
  region. This helps us keep the site reliable and understand which learning
  flows need improvement.
- **Payment information**: If paid plans are enabled, payments are processed by a
  third-party payment provider. We do not store full card numbers on Lang Study
  servers.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide character practice, worksheets, accounts, and related product features
- keep users signed in and protect accounts
- save learning progress where the feature supports it
- respond to support, feedback, and partnership messages
- understand product usage, diagnose errors, and improve the learning experience
- manage paid access if subscriptions or lifetime purchases are enabled
- comply with legal, security, and abuse-prevention obligations

## Browser Storage and Cookies

Lang Study may use cookies, local storage, or similar browser storage for sign-in
sessions, theme preferences, language settings, learning progress, and product
reliability. You can clear browser storage in your browser settings, but doing so
may remove local lesson progress or sign you out.

## Third-Party Services

Lang Study may rely on third-party services to run the product:

- **Cloudflare** for hosting, Workers, security, logs, and site analytics
- **Hanzi Writer and Hanzi Writer data**, loaded through public CDN resources,
  for stroke-order animation and guided tracing
- **GitHub** for the public source repository and issue tracking
- **Email providers** if transactional or support email is enabled
- **Payment providers** if paid plans are enabled

These services process information according to their own policies.

## Data Retention

We keep account and product data for as long as needed to provide Lang Study,
protect the service, comply with legal obligations, and support reasonable
business records. You may request deletion of account-related data by contacting
us.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data.
No online service can guarantee perfect security, so please use a strong password
and contact us if you believe your account or data has been affected.

## Children's Privacy

Lang Study may be useful for families and classrooms, but children should use it
with a parent, guardian, teacher, or school administrator where required by local
law.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Lang Study adds new learning, account, or
commercial features. The latest version will be posted on this page with an
updated date.

## Contact Us

Questions about privacy or data handling can be sent to
[support@getlangstudy.com](mailto:support@getlangstudy.com), or through the
[contact page](/contact).
