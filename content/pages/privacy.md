## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how ClassGamify ("ClassGamify", "we", "us", or
"our") handles personal information when you use our classroom activity
platform, including activity authoring, game-style templates, assignment links,
student runners, worksheets, AI-assisted tools, accounts, support, file uploads,
and paid features if they are enabled.

This policy is intended to describe the product as it operates today and as paid
classroom features are introduced. If a checkout page, payment processor, or
merchant of record provides additional privacy terms, those terms apply to the
payment transaction in addition to this policy.

## Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide, secure, support, and improve
ClassGamify.

- **Account and authentication information**: If you create an account, we may
  collect your name, email address, password authentication data, email
  verification status, session records, linked OAuth provider information such
  as Google sign-in identifiers, account role, ban status, and related security
  records.
- **Classroom activity content**: We may process activity titles, descriptions,
  teacher instructions, learning goals, vocabulary, questions, answer keys,
  accepted alternatives, answer explanations, matching pairs, groups, listening
  scripts, worksheet settings, selected templates, assignment settings, and
  public share identifiers.
- **Student participation data**: When a student uses an assignment link, we may
  process the student name if the teacher chooses to collect it, an anonymous
  browser token for attempt limits, submitted answers, score, accuracy,
  completion time, attempt duration, review state, and technical data needed to
  show the student result and teacher results.
- **Files and profile content**: If account file, avatar, audio, image, or
  worksheet upload features are available and you upload files, we process the
  file name, type, size, storage key, upload time, and the file itself.
- **AI prompts, uploads, and generated results**: If you use AI-assisted
  drafting, remixing, distractor generation, answer explanations, listening
  scripts, worksheet extraction, image generation, or similar tools, we may
  process the text you enter, uploaded images or files, and generated output
  through the configured provider. Depending on the feature, those inputs and
  outputs may also be logged for abuse prevention, debugging, or rate limiting.
- **Contact and support information**: If you email us, submit a contact form,
  or use a support channel, we receive the contact details and message content
  you choose to provide.
- **Payment and billing information**: If paid plans are enabled, payment
  processors such as Creem or Stripe may collect payment details, billing
  address, tax information, transaction identifiers, subscription status,
  invoices, and fraud-prevention information. ClassGamify does not store full
  card numbers on its own servers.
- **Usage, analytics, and device data**: We may process page views, referrers,
  browser and device type, approximate region, IP address, request logs, error
  logs, product interactions, performance data, and similar technical data.
  Analytics tools may include services such as Cloudflare analytics, Umami,
  Microsoft Clarity, Google Analytics, or Plausible, depending on configuration.
- **Cookies and local storage**: We use cookies, local storage, and similar
  technologies for authentication, language preference, theme preference,
  sidebar state, assignment tokens, local runner state, security, analytics, and
  product reliability.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide classroom activity creation, templates, assignments, student runners,
  worksheets, accounts, paid access, support, and related product features
- authenticate users, keep sessions secure, verify email addresses, and prevent
  account abuse
- publish assignment links, enforce assignment settings, score attempts, show
  student feedback where enabled, and provide teacher result summaries
- generate, remix, or improve activity drafts requested by the user
- process purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, invoices, and payment
  support when paid features are enabled
- respond to support, feedback, partnership, and legal requests
- understand product usage, diagnose errors, measure conversion, and improve the
  classroom experience
- protect ClassGamify, users, students, and third parties from fraud, spam,
  security incidents, and misuse
- comply with legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations

Where laws such as the GDPR or UK GDPR apply, our legal bases may include
performance of a contract, legitimate interests, consent where required, and
compliance with legal obligations.

## Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for
cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are commonly used in U.S.
state privacy laws.

We may share information with service providers that help us operate
ClassGamify, including:

- **Cloudflare** for hosting, Workers, D1 database, R2 storage, DNS, security,
  logs, email sending, and analytics
- **Better Auth and OAuth providers** for account authentication, including
  Google sign-in if enabled
- **Cloudflare Workers AI, fal.ai, and similar AI providers** for AI-assisted
  drafting, text generation, image generation, worksheet extraction, or related
  experimental features when those tools are enabled
- **Analytics providers** such as Microsoft Clarity, Umami, Google Analytics, or
  Plausible, when configured
- **Payment providers** such as Creem or Stripe, when paid checkout is enabled
- **Email and support providers** for transactional email, verification email,
  password reset email, and support communication
- **Storage providers** such as Cloudflare R2 for uploaded files, if file
  features are enabled
- **Professional advisers or authorities** where necessary for legal, security,
  tax, compliance, or dispute purposes

These providers process information under their own terms and privacy policies.
We try to use providers that are appropriate for a small online education
product and that support reasonable security practices.

## Classroom and Student Data

Teachers, tutors, schools, and organizations decide what classroom content they
enter, which assignment settings they enable, whether student names are
collected, and which students receive public assignment links. If you use
ClassGamify with students, you are responsible for obtaining any permissions
required by your organization, school, family, or applicable law.

Do not ask students to submit sensitive personal information through assignment
answers, student name fields, comments, or uploads. Student results are intended
for classroom review, reteaching, feedback, and ordinary instructional records.

## Cookies, Local Storage, and Analytics

Some storage is essential for the service to work, such as authentication
cookies, language preference, theme preference, assignment attempt tokens, and
security-related data. Local runner state may be stored in your browser so an
assignment attempt can continue on the same device where appropriate.

You can clear cookies or local storage in your browser settings. Clearing them
may sign you out, reset preferences, remove local runner state, or affect
anonymous assignment attempt limits.

Analytics tools help us understand whether teachers and students can find, use,
and complete the product flows. Depending on the provider, analytics may collect
page URLs, browser details, device details, interaction events, approximate
location, and technical identifiers. We use this data to improve reliability,
usability, and product direction, not to sell personal information.

## Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes
described in this policy:

- account records are kept while your account is active, and for a reasonable
  period afterward for security, backup, legal, or audit needs
- activity, assignment, attempt, and result records are kept while the teacher
  account or classroom workflow needs them, unless deleted through available
  product controls or support processes
- payment and invoice records may be retained for tax, accounting, fraud
  prevention, and legal obligations
- support messages are retained for as long as needed to respond and maintain
  reasonable business records
- security logs and technical logs are kept for limited periods unless needed to
  investigate abuse or incidents
- browser-local storage stays on your device until you clear it or the browser
  removes it

## Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete,
export, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information.
You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on
consent.

You can:

- update or delete account information through account settings where available
- delete your account where the product provides account deletion
- edit or delete classroom content where product controls are available
- clear local browser storage to remove local assignment or preference data
- contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of account data
- opt out of non-essential analytics through browser settings, privacy tools, or
  provider-level controls where available

We may need to verify your request before acting on it. We may keep certain
information where required for security, legal, tax, accounting, dispute, or
fraud-prevention reasons.

## Children's Privacy

ClassGamify is designed for teachers, tutors, schools, and authorized classroom
use. It is not directed to unsupervised use by children under 13. Children and
students should use ClassGamify with a parent, guardian, teacher, tutor, school,
or administrator where required by local law.

If you are under 13, do not create an account or provide personal information
unless a parent, guardian, school, or other authorized adult has approved your
use where legally required. If we learn that we collected personal information
from a child under 13 without required consent, we will take reasonable steps to
delete it. Parents, guardians, and schools can contact us at the email below.

## International Processing

ClassGamify is available online and may use service providers in different
countries. Your information may be processed in countries other than where you
live. Where required, we rely on appropriate legal mechanisms, provider
commitments, and security safeguards for international processing.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal
information, including authentication controls, access controls, provider
security features, and HTTPS. No online service can guarantee perfect security.
Please use a strong password and contact us if you believe your account or data
has been affected.

material, we will take reasonable steps to provide additional notice where
appropriate.
